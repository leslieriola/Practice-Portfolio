About Me

Hi, I'm Leslie Ann Riola. I'm a book author and a Web developer. Writing has been my passion since my school days. I won an on the spot essay writing contest inside UP Diliman and got featured in the Global News Section in The Philippine Star Newspaper. Ukiyoto Publishing Company provides me the opportunity to write for them. I now have 6 books in Ukiyoto, and 2 books in UP Diliman Library.
